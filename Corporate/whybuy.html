Why Buy Hospitality?

The Corporate Hospitality industry has grown to be a $4bilion a year business world wide. This growth is attributed to the highly competitive nature of modern business where corporate clients and employees are now far more demanding. Corporate hospitality has replaced traditional business practice of customer and client retention to become a useful tool for growing businesses, retaining clients, motivating and rewarding staff as well as an excellent day out.

Clients

Hospitality has become increasingly important as the cost of customer acquisition has soared. It now cost about four or five times as much to acquire a new client as it does to keep an existing client. Faces-to-face hospitality now has a vital role to play in developing and sustaining customer relationships within business. Recent analysis concludes that for most organizations, 80% of their business is attributable to only 20% of their client base. Therefore maintaining profitable client relationships, increasing the account penetration with new products or services and have a multi-layer client contact strategy is a very attractive one option. The lifetime profitability of a customer has become even more important rather than the short termist view of simply making the sale. Organisations have become increasingly focused and quality orientated. Distributing 'champagne hospitality' and inviting large numbers of people on company junkets have been left behind in favour of more targeted, carefully planned and bespoke events.

  Employees

Hand-in-hand with this strategy of client retention has been employee retention. Companies value thier employees and much hard work has to be done in order to train and provide employees with the necessary skills and capabilities to perform effectively and efficiently in their job. People want to feel a sense of belonging, especially in uncertain climates; therefore employee participation in hospitality is a key tool in employee retention as well as making sure employees are less receptive to overtures from a competitor. Our clients report tangible benefits from entertaining at Premier Lifestyle hosted events in terms of building strong and lasting business relationships. In addition, they provide an excellent reason to meet business associates face to face whilst enjoying a shared interest and presenting your company in a positive light.
