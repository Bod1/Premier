Premier Lifestyle has teamed up with upmarket Karma Resorts to offer members privileges at the following luxurious resorts:

  










Karma Samui, Koh Samui, Thailand

Location: Koh Samui is a picturesque island off the south east coast of Thailand, home to waterfalls, mountains, jungle and glorious sandy beaches. PLL has negotiated special rates at Karma Samui, a luxury resort situated on a stunning tropical beach, just five minutes’ drive from Chaweng Beach, the island’s main shopping and entertainment centre with designer shops, chic clubs and restaurants. It’s also just 15 minutes from the international airport.

Accommodation: Each one of the exquisite 36 one-, two-, three- and four-bedroom pool residences combines elements of contemporary and traditional Thai architecture furnished to high standards with Thai silks and handcrafted artworks. Residences have fully-equipped kitchens, TV/DVD, wireless internet connections, en-suite bathrooms, tropical outdoor shower and, of course, a stunning infinity pool. Some boast enviable locations on the beachfront where guests can wander barefoot onto the glorious stretch of sand and wade into clear emerald waters. An array of personal chefs and waiters are on standby to make every guest’s dream come true.

Facilities: Dine in Padma for explosive, mouth-watering modern Thai cuisine in chic surroundings or relax in the Chakra Spa where you can experience all manner of traditional Thai and Ayurvedic treatments. Other facilities include an outdoor pool and fitness centre.

Excursions: Ultimate adventures include sea kayaking around marine parks to see picturesque coves, bizarre rock formations and caves. Or take a jungle excursion in a four-wheel drive jeep to visit magnificent mountains and waterfalls. Explore and shop for souvenirs in nearby Chaweng Beach or take a trip to a deserted beach and fish for your dinner. Other ‘must-sees’ include a Thai boxing match; Big Buddha, a 12-metre high statue; and the full moon party that takes place once a month on the nearby island Ko Pha Ngan.

Top tips: Join the resort’s private Thai street food cooking class which takes in street markets to taste hawker food and teaches you how to recreate authentic Thai food at home.

Need to know more: See www.karmasamui.com		  



 



  Karma Jimbaran, Bali, Indonesia

Location: The beautiful island of Bali beguiles all who visit with its sweeping sandy beaches, exquisite food, stunning scenery and magnificent temples. Ideal for those looking for a laid-back holiday in exotic, sophisticated surroundings. PLL has negotiated an impressive discount for members at this luxurious resort, nestling in the heart of Jimbaran, Bali, just 5km from the international airport (10-15 minutes by taxi).

Accommodation: Stay in one of 38 spacious, luxury villas or residences, all contemporary, yet carefully crafted with traditional Bali-influenced décor and design. Each residence boasts a private pool set in a tropical garden where you can relax or soak up the sun in total seclusion as the fragrant frangipani wafts around you. A true sanctuary of serene sophistication.

Facilities: Take a plunge in the main pool by the restaurant or visit the acclaimed spa where you can choose from an impressive spa menu including a variety of massages with specially blended oils to help relax and move energy through the seven Chakras. Dine in the delectable restaurant to sample local specialities, or sip a martini in the bar while watching the sun set.

Excursions: A variety of excursions are available including elephant rides, white-water rafting, swimming with dolphins, scuba diving, deep sea fishing, sunset cruises and cooking classes. A selection of sports are also on offer nearby such as golf, horse riding and learn to surf. Or simply take a tour to see Bali's utterly breathtaking views: white sandy beaches, towering volcanoes, carefully carved rice terraces, fast-flowing rivers and lush forests teaming with bird and wildlife.

Top tips: Don’t miss a trip to the cultural centre of Ubud with its stunning handicrafts and backdrop of rice paddies. Sunset from Tanah Lot temple, built on a rugged outcrop in the sea.

Need to know more? See www.karmajimbaran.com
